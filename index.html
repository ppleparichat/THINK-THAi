Our Books

Unlock the Beauty and Complexity of Thai Language with I Can Speak Thai Books I & II

Speaking & Listening

Authored by our expert Thai language instructors, these books are a comprehensive guide to mastering conversational Thai. Perfect for self-study beginners and language instructors, the I Can Speak Thai series includes 20 lessons that focus on essential vocabulary, sentence structures, culture, and more through phonetics for more natural pronunciations. Start your journey to fluency today with Sumaa Language and Culture Institute's I Can Speak Thai Books I & II

Reading & Writing

Designed by Sumaa Language and Culture Institute's expert Thai language instructors, I Can Read Thai (Book I & II) is the ultimate guide to mastering the Thai alphabet and writing system. This book provides a systematic approach to learning how to read consonants and vowels and includes a variety of reading exercises to help you check your understanding of tone rules and ensure that your reading skills are on track. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced Thai language learner, I Can Read Thai (Book I & II) is an indispensable tool for unlocking the power of Thai language reading.
